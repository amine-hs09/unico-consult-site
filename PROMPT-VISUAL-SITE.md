## PROMPT (copier tout ce qui suit)

---

Design a complete, premium corporate website for **Unico Consult**, a Belgian business management consulting firm founded in 2019. The site must look **light, elegant, and corporate** — white/cream backgrounds with refined purple-blue accents. NO dark mode, NO dark backgrounds. The design must feel **boutique premium** — like a high-end consulting firm that handles everything so entrepreneurs can focus on their passion.

### BRAND IDENTITY

- **Company:** Unico Consult — "Votre business, notre challenge"
- **Industry:** Business management consulting (NOT an IT company, NOT a tech startup)
- **Founded:** 2019, Belgium
- **Location:** Avenue Hippocrate 4, 1932 Sint-Stevens-Woluwe (near Brussels), Belgium
- **Team:** 3 senior consultants (Otman Hssinoui — GM/Finance, Farid Saouti — Director/HR/Legal, Jamal El Kadaoui — Operations/Logistics)
- **Tagline options:** "Votre business, notre challenge" / "Ensemble, concrétisons votre vision" / "Concentrez-vous sur votre passion, on s'occupe du reste"

### COLOR PALETTE — STRICT

- **Primary:** Deep purple/mauve (#7C3AED or similar — the brand signature color)
- **Secondary:** Royal blue (#2563EB or similar)
- **Accent:** Soft lavender/lilac for subtle backgrounds (#F5F3FF, #EDE9FE)
- **Text:** Deep charcoal (#1E1B4B) on white
- **Backgrounds:** Pure white (#FFFFFF) and warm off-white/cream (#FAFAF9, #F8F7FC)
- **NO teal, NO green, NO orange** — purple and blue ONLY
- **NO dark sections** — everything stays light and airy

### DESIGN DIRECTION — CRITICAL

This site must NOT look like:
- ❌ A generic Wix/Squarespace AI-generated template
- ❌ A tech startup landing page with gradients and 3D blobs
- ❌ A boring corporate site with stock photos of handshakes
- ❌ A "consultant" site that looks like every other consultant site

This site MUST look like:
- ✅ A premium boutique consulting firm — think McKinsey meets Belgian elegance
- ✅ Clean, spacious, lots of white space — content breathes
- ✅ Sophisticated typography — strong hierarchy, large display headings, refined body text
- ✅ Subtle animations that convey competence and precision (not flashy)
- ✅ Custom geometric patterns or abstract shapes as decorative elements (not stock photos)
- ✅ Purple used sparingly but with impact — accent lines, hover states, key CTAs
- ✅ A site where you immediately feel: "these people are serious professionals"

### TYPOGRAPHY RECOMMENDATIONS

- Display/headings: Plus Jakarta Sans, Inter, or Satoshi (modern geometric sans-serif, bold weight)
- Body text: Heebo, Inter, or DM Sans (clean, highly readable)
- Numbers/stats: Tabular figures, oversized for impact
- French language — ensure proper character rendering (accents, cédilles)

### UNIQUE DESIGN ELEMENTS TO INCLUDE

1. **Geometric brand pattern** — a subtle repeating pattern using angular/hexagonal shapes derived from the "U" in Unico, used as section backgrounds and dividers
2. **Animated number counters** — the key figures animate when scrolling into view
3. **Hover micro-interactions** — service cards lift with subtle shadow, logos colorize, buttons pulse gently
4. **Smooth scroll transitions** — sections fade in as user scrolls
5. **Purple accent lines** — thin decorative lines that separate sections or accent headings
6. **Custom iconography** — consistent line-art icon set in purple for all 5 services and 4 sectors
7. **Testimonial carousel** — auto-rotating with manual navigation dots
8. **Breadcrumb navigation** — on inner pages for SEO and UX

### RESPONSIVE DESIGN

- Desktop: 1440px max width, generous padding
- Tablet: stack columns, adjust typography scale
- Mobile: single column, hamburger nav, touch-friendly CTAs (min 48px tap targets)
- All breakpoints should feel intentionally designed, not just "shrunk"

### WHAT MAKES THIS SITE DIFFERENT FROM OTHER CONSULTING FIRMS

1. **Real client diversity visible** — 13+ logos spanning cleaning, transport, restaurants, healthcare, real estate — proves versatility
2. **No stock photos** — abstract art, geometric patterns, clean typography carry the design
3. **Belgian identity** — subtle nods to Belgian business culture, TVA number prominent, bilingual awareness
4. **Warmth despite corporate** — the testimonials and "votre passion" messaging add humanity
5. **UnicoWorking** — a physical meeting space you can book, adding tangibility that pure consultants lack
6. **Concrete numbers** — not vague promises, but ~15 years, 15+ clients, 5 poles, 4 sectors
