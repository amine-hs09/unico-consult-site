## PROMPT 1 — Homepage Full (Desktop, Above the fold + scroll)

Create a ultra high-fidelity full-page website mockup for a premium Belgian consulting firm called "Unico Consult". This is a realistic UI design, pixel-perfect, as seen in a Figma or Dribbble presentation.

**Style reference:** Combine the elegance of Deloitte.com with the clean minimalism of Linear.app and the whitespace of Apple.com. Light mode only. No dark backgrounds anywhere.

**Brand colors:**
- Primary: deep purple #7C3AED (used sparingly for accents, buttons, and highlights)
- Background: pure white #FFFFFF and soft lavender #F8F7FC
- Text: dark navy #1E1B4B
- Secondary accent: soft blue #818CF8
- No green, no teal, no orange

**Typography:** Modern geometric sans-serif (like Plus Jakarta Sans or Satoshi). Very large bold headlines (72px+), light refined body text. French language.

**Layout — scroll from top to bottom:**

**[SECTION 1 — HERO]**
Sticky white navigation bar at top: logo "Unico Consult" on left (text logo, purple wordmark), menu links center (Expertise · Secteurs · Clients · Équipe · Contact), purple "Nous contacter" button right.
Below: massive centered headline "Concentrez-vous sur votre passion" in dark navy, 80px font. Subtitle below in gray: "Expert en accompagnement de gestion d'entreprise · Bruxelles, Belgique". Two buttons centered: "Découvrir nos services" (solid purple, rounded) and "Nous contacter" (outlined purple). Background: clean white with a very subtle abstract geometric pattern made of thin purple lines in the top-right corner. No photos. No illustrations of people.

**[SECTION 2 — KEY FIGURES]**
Horizontal strip with soft lavender #F8F7FC background. 6 large animated-style number counters in a row:
"15 ans d'expérience" · "15+ clients" · "5 pôles de services" · "4 secteurs" · "3 experts" · "100% sur mesure"
Numbers displayed very large (48px) in purple, labels below in small gray text.

**[SECTION 3 — 5 SERVICES]**
White background. Section title "Nos pôles d'expertise" centered in large bold text.
5 elegant cards in a row, each with: a minimal purple line-art icon at top, service name in bold, one line of description, small purple arrow "En savoir plus →". Cards have subtle rounded borders, light shadow on hover effect.
Services: Ressources Humaines · Gestion & Management · Back-office & Outsourcing · Logistique · Image de marque

**[SECTION 4 — CLIENT LOGOS]**
Section title "Ils nous font confiance" centered. Below: a horizontal scrolling band of 13 client company logos displayed in elegant monochrome grayscale. Logos evenly spaced. Clean white background.

**[SECTION 5 — TESTIMONIALS]**
Soft lavender background. 3 testimonial cards side by side. Each card: large purple opening quote mark " at top, italic testimonial text, then client name in bold and company name + sector in gray below. Cards have white background, rounded corners, subtle shadow.

**[SECTION 6 — CTA BANNER]**
Full-width section with a soft purple gradient background (#7C3AED to #818CF8, very subtle). White centered text: "Prêt à vous concentrer sur votre passion ?" with a large white button "Contactez-nous".

**[SECTION 7 — FOOTER]**
Dark navy #1E1B4B footer (only dark element). White text. 4 columns: Company info (logo + address + phone + email), Navigation links, Services links, Legal links. Bottom bar: "© 2019-2026 Unico Consult SRL · TVA BE 0735.798.943"

**Output:** One single full-page screenshot-style mockup showing the entire homepage scrolled out, desktop 1440px wide, photorealistic UI design quality. No wireframes. No sketches. This should look like a finished, shippable website design.
